Welcome to IOM Migration Support

The International Organization for Migration (IOM) works to help ensure the orderly and humane management of migration, to promote international cooperation on migration issues, to assist in the search for practical solutions to migration problems and to provide humanitarian assistance to migrants in need.

Our Key Services

  • Migration Assistance
  • Humanitarian Support
  • Migration Health
  • Labor Migration
  • Counter-Trafficking
